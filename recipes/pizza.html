Pizza

Description

Pizza is a beloved dish that originated in Italy and has become popular worldwide. It typically consists of a round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and various other ingredients such as meats, vegetables, and herbs, then baked at a high temperature until the crust is crispy and the cheese is melted and bubbly.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions)
  • Olive Oil
  • Herbs and Spices (e.g., oregano, basil, garlic powder)

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
  2. Preheat the oven
  3. Roll out the dough
  4. Add the sauce, cheese, and toppings
  5. Bake the pizza
  6. Slice and serve
