Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic italian baked dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with a rich combination of meat or vegetable sauce, creamy ricotta, and plenty of melted cheese usually mozzarella and Parmesan.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Cheeses
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Herbs and Spices
  • Egg
  • Onion and Garlic

Steps

  1. Make a meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
